STORY OF MERIT

MERIT is a minimalist beauty brand founded by Katherine Power in 2021, designed as an antidote to the overwhelming world of beauty. The brand focuses on simplifying routines by offering well-edited essentials that allow individuals to achieve more with less. MERIT's products are clean, vegan, cruelty-free, and formulated with skin-loving ingredients to support skin health even after makeup removal. Emphasizing intentionality, MERIT spends years perfecting each product to ensure it seamlessly integrates into daily life. This commitment to simplicity and quality has positioned MERIT as a leader in the shift towards minimalist beauty.
